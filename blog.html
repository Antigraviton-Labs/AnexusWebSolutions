10 Apr 2026

ThalliTrack Officially Deployed – Testing Phase Begins

We are excited to announce that our SaaS application ThalliTrack, developed under Anexus Web Solutions, has been officially deployed and is now live for testing.

Starting from 10 April 2026, we have begun the mess registration process on the platform. ThalliTrack is designed to solve a real-world problem faced by students every day — deciding where and what to eat. At the same time, it helps mess owners bring their services online and connect with more students easily.

Currently, we are in the testing phase, where we are improving features, collecting feedback, and making the platform more reliable and user-friendly. This phase is very important to ensure a smooth and effective experience for both students and mess owners.

ThalliTrack aims to create a simple, fast, and smart system where students can explore nearby mess options, check daily menus, and make quick decisions, while mess owners can manage their menu and visibility digitally.

We are working continuously to refine the platform and will soon be launching the final version across Pune city.

This is just the beginning of building something impactful, and we are excited to take ThalliTrack to the next level 🚀
